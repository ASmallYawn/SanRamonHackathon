Know where you go.

Being safe from Covid is a huge concern for many people planning to go on vacation during this pandemic. That's why we created this app to help people know which places have a higher risk of contracting the virus from. Simply search for the city that you want to visit, and the app will provide you with a risk score that is calculated using data from multiple sources, such as covidactnow.org and the CDC.
